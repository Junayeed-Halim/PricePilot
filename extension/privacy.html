✈ PricePilot AU

Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 2026
Short version: We don't sell your data, don't track your browsing history, and don't create profiles about you. The extension reads product prices on the pages you visit and nothing else.

What PricePilot AU does

PricePilot AU is a Chrome extension that detects product pages you visit and shows you real-time prices from other Australian retailers, so you can quickly find the best deal without visiting each store manually.

Data stored locally on your device

The following is stored in Chrome's local extension storage (on your device only, never sent to us):

  • Anthropic API key — if you choose to enable AI features. Sent directly from your browser to Anthropic's API and never seen by us.
  • Backend URL — the address of the price-scraping server you configure.
  • Price alert targets — product names and target prices you set for price drop alerts.

Data sent to the backend server

When you use the extension, the product name from the page you are viewing is sent to the configured backend server to search for prices. If you set a price drop alert and provide an email address:

  • Your email address, the product name, and your target price are stored on the backend server.
  • This data is used only to send you a price drop notification email. It is not shared with any third party.
  • You can delete your alerts at any time from the extension popup.

Browsing data

PricePilot AU does not:

  • Track which websites you visit.
  • Record your browsing history.
  • Transmit any page content beyond the product name and price visible on the current page.
  • Use cookies or fingerprinting.
  • Sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-party services

  • Anthropic — if you provide a Claude API key, your chat messages and product context are sent to Anthropic to generate AI responses. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Retailer websites — the extension reads publicly available product and price information from retailer pages you visit. No account or personal data is shared with retailers.

Data retention

Price alert data (email + product + target price) is retained on the backend server until you delete the alert. No purchase history or browsing history is ever stored.

Children

PricePilot AU is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
